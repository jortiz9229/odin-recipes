Lasagna

Description

Ah, Lasagna, world famous for its iconically layered assortment of meat, cheese, and square noodles. An Italian layer cake made of cheesy pasta, no wonder a certain comic strip featuring an orange cat is famous for his love of the dish.

Follow this recipe to discover why the dish is so iconic.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather Ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.

  3. Step 3

    4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

  5. Step 5

    6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  6. Step 6

    7. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  7. Step 7

    8. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

  8. Step 8

    9. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  9. Step 9

    10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

  10. Step 10

    11. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
