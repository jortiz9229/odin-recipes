Tall Glass of Water

Description

A tall glass of water is both refreshing and needed for life, making this recipe essential to making sure your journey around the sun happens more than once, or even twice! Once you have a glass of this refreshing combination of atomic gasses, you'll surely be back for more. Follow this recipe closely to make sure your carefully crafted glass of water is both wet, and void of nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounce cup, preferrably made of glass, ceramic, or plastic
  • potable water source
  • water dispensing aparatus
  • ice cubes, to taste
  • solid platform to rest cup on

Directions

  1. Take your chosen glass, and place it on your solid platform, make sure the glass is clean.

  2. Take your water dispensing aparatus and fill it with cold water from your potable source, usually a kitchen faucet, a bathroom faucet also works, but is typically frowned upon.

  3. Tilting your water dispensing aparatus at an angle ensuring a steady controlled flow, pour the water into your chosen glass until the water level is about 1/4 inch below the rim. You may use a ruler if you so wish. Place your water dis;ensing aparatus on the solid platform beside your cup.

  4. Take your ice cubes and drop them in to taste, you may wish to wait a couple minutes to allow the water to cool even more.

  5. Enjoy!
