Chocolate Milk

Description

Chocolate is loved by millions, and millions also love the creamy texture of a sugary dessert. Thus born, was milk chocolate, a candy bar made with roasted cacao, and fatty bovine milk, creating a decadent dessert many would give to their spouse along with flowers after making a poor decision. But, what if you swapped those words around? Rather than adding milk to chocolate, why don't we add chocolate to milk. BAM, here you have a toddler's dream, the fabled chocolate milk.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce cup
  • 12 ounces whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 3 teaspoons sugar
  • spoon to mix

Directions

  1. Carefully pour milk into cup.

  2. Measure and pour cocoa and sugar into cup.

  3. Use spoon to vigorously stir in cocoa and sugar.

  4. Enjoy!
